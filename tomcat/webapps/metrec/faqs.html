    What is MetRec?

    MetRec is a database of Metabolic Reconstructions.The purpose of the MetRec server is to host a repository of carefully curated high-quality GSMs(genome-scale metabolic models) of pathogenic organisms.

    How is it different from other such metabolic model databases?

    The lack of usage of standard nomenclature for the metabolites and reactions makes it difficult to compare and match similar metabolic pathways across different models and different databases. To address this gap, we created a new platform which houses metabolic models of pathogenic organisms in a systematized fashion.
